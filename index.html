About Us

✨ Crafted with Love, Served with Pride ✨

Laghu Bhojan, we believe that food is more than just nourishment—it’s a celebration of taste, tradition, and love. We are small, home-based startup dedicated to making sweets and savories. Each of our treats is made with a commitment to authenticity, quality, and the kind of taste that feels like home. we craft every items treat with the utmost care, using only the finest ingredients to bring authentic, wholesome flavors straight to your table. Every bite you take is a reflection of our passion for quality and authenticity. We never compromise on taste or health—we use no palm oil, and we ensure that every snack is made fresh, without repeatedly using oils. Whether you’re craving something sweet or savory, you can trust that our food is made with the highest standards and a whole lot of heart. Indulge in fresh, authentic snacks that bring the flavors of tradition and the warmth of home to every moment.

✨ Our Promise ✨

When you choose Laghu Bhojan, you’re choosing a brand that stands firmly against shortcuts. We’re proud to offer 100% homemade food, made fresh to order. Our ingredients are carefully selected, and we never compromise on quality. We guarantee: • No Palm Oil – We believe in healthy, natural oils that contribute to both great taste and a healthy lifestyle. • No Reused Oils – Freshness matters, and so does the integrity of our food. We never reuse oils, ensuring that each snack is crisp, clean, and packed with flavor. • Fresh, Handcrafted Snacks – Every snack is made to order, ensuring you get the freshest, most delicious experience possible. Whether it’s a sweet indulgence or a savory delight, you can count on us for a treat made from scratch.

✨ Why We Do It ✨

Our journey started with a simple love for food and a desire to share that joy with others. We understand the importance of food that nourishes the body and comforts the soul. That’s why we’re dedicated to creating snacks that not only taste amazing but also bring the authentic flavors and essence of home-cooked meals to your table.

✨ Authentic Taste, Every Time✨

From traditional family recipes to unique creations, every bite is a reminder of the rich flavors passed down through generations. Our snacks have that typical, authentic taste you’ve been craving—deliciously satisfying, with a touch of nostalgia. Every treat is lovingly prepared to deliver the same quality and taste that we would serve our own family.

• Sweets – From indulgent, melt-in-your-mouth delights to traditional treats, our sweets are a celebration of flavor and craftsmanship. • Savories – Whether you’re craving something crispy, crunchy, or spicy, our savory snacks are made with a perfect balance of seasoning and texture. • Instant Podis – Traditional homemade spice blends that add rich flavour and aroma to your meals instantly.
